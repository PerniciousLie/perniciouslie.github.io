Meet the Developer

    PerniciousLie was going to College for an Associates in Software Development. Their interest in Solar Updraft Towers began in high school (12 years ago) and they are suprised the green energy lobbyists haven't tried pitching this technology to congress. The issue surrounding the implementation of SUTs in arid regions for terraforming purposes is the cost of building such a large structure, but the towers will pay off the incurred cost of construction in 3-4 years (which is better than solar panels which take 10 or more years to pay for themselves and require constant maintenence).
