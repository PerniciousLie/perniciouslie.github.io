Solar updraft towers are an unknown alternative of green energy.

The solar updraft tower (SUT) is a design concept for a renewable-energy power plant for generating electricity from low temperature solar heat. Sunlight heats the air beneath a very wide greenhouse-roofed structure surrounding the central base of a very tall chimney tower.
